# Know Thy Enemy

**1. THE ALGORITHM PLEASERS**

- **Identity:** Traditional agencies with digital departments
- **Tactics:** Data-driven content optimization, trend-chasing, engagement hacking
- **Weakness:** So busy optimizing for machines they forgot about humans
- **Battle Cry:** "Our engagement metrics are up 3.7% month-over-month!"

**2. THE PRODUCTION MACHINERY**

- **Identity:** Production houses churning out polished mediocrity
- **Tactics:** Beautiful emptiness, technical perfection, style over substance
- **Weakness:** Making things pretty doesn't make them matter
- **Battle Cry:** "Yes! We have a template ready for this"

**3. THE CONTENT CONVEYOR BELT**

- **Identity:** Content farms focused on volume and velocity
- **Tactics:** Repurposing, recycling, remixing the same ideas
- **Weakness:** Quantity has a quality all its own – the quality of being forgettable
- **Battle Cry:** "We posted 47 times this week!"

**4. THE TECH EVANGELISTS**

- **Identity:** Tech companies who think creativity is a feature, not a foundation
- **Tactics:** AI-generated everything, automation over imagination
- **Weakness:** Tools without vision just build prettier versions of the same thing
- **Battle Cry:** "Our AI can generate 1,000 social posts in 30 seconds!"

**5. THE INFLUENCER INDUSTRIAL COMPLEX**

- **Identity:** Creator economy players who've industrialized authenticity
- **Tactics:** Personality as product, manufactured relatability, performance art as reality
- **Weakness:** When everyone's authentic the same way, no one actually is
- **Battle Cry:** "I'm just being real with you guys!"
